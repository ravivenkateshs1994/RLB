Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026  ·  Rich Land Builders, Coimbatore

Thank you for visiting Rich Land Builders. We want you to know that your privacy matters to us. This policy explains what information we collect, why we collect it, and how we use and protect it when you browse our website or submit an enquiry.

This policy applies to our website, contact form, brochure requests, and any follow-up communication that results from them.

Collection and Use of Information

General

In general, you can browse our website without telling us who you are. When you choose to contact us or request a brochure, we may receive the information you submit through the website, by email, or through the Google services that help us process enquiries.

Information you provide

When you submit a form, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Message or enquiry details
  • Project interest or enquiry type
  • Source page or form label, such as Homepage, Address page, or Contact page
  • Any other information you include in the form

We do not knowingly request payment card details, government ID numbers, or other sensitive personal data through this website.

Use of Your Personal Information

We may use the information we collect to:

  • Respond to your enquiry and share the project information you requested
  • Send brochures, pricing, or follow-up details
  • Contact you by email, phone, SMS, or WhatsApp about your enquiry if you have given consent
  • Understand which pages generate enquiries so we can improve the website experience
  • Prevent spam, fraud, and abuse
  • Meet legal, accounting, and record-keeping requirements

We do not use your personal information for unrelated advertising or automated profiling.

Personal Information that we May Share with Others

Service Providers

We use Google reCAPTCHA to reduce spam and abuse, our form-processing system to receive form submissions and store enquiry records, and Google Fonts or Google Maps when those features are loaded on the site. We only share the information needed for each of these services to function.

Compliance with law and fraud protection

We may disclose information when we believe it is necessary to comply with law, protect against fraud or abuse, or enforce our rights.

Business transfer

If Rich Land Builders is involved in a merger, acquisition, restructuring, or sale of assets, the information we hold may be transferred as part of that transaction.

Other Uses of Your Information

IP address and device information

When you visit the website, we may receive your IP address and basic device or browser details to help diagnose problems, understand traffic patterns, and keep the site secure.

Cookies and similar technologies

We do not run advertising pixels or marketing cookies on our site. However, Google reCAPTCHA, Google Maps, and Google Fonts may use cookies or similar technologies as part of their normal operation. Any third-party page you visit is governed by that provider’s privacy policy.

Aggregated information

We may use non-identifiable aggregate information to understand how our site is used and to improve the experience for visitors.

Accessing and Updating Your Information

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of the personal information we hold about you, subject to applicable law. To make a request, email rlbenquiry@gmail.com.

We keep enquiry records only for as long as needed to respond to your request, maintain records, comply with law, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

Choice / Opt-Out

If you no longer want to hear from us about a specific enquiry, you can ask us to stop non-essential communication by emailing us. Where you have given consent, you may also withdraw it for future communications.

Children's privacy

Our website is intended for adults interested in residential property enquiries. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Links

Our website may link to Google Maps, social media profiles, and other external sites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their policies.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organisational safeguards to protect the information we hold. However, no internet transmission or storage system can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page will reflect the current version. Continued use of the website after a change means you accept the updated policy.
