Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026  ·  Rich Land Builders, Coimbatore

1. Who we are

Rich Land Builders (RLB) is a residential construction company based in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu, India. Our registered office is at JV Avenue PHASE - 3, Thirumurugan Nagar, Kalapatti, Coimbatore – 641048. You can reach us at info@richlandbuilders.com or on +91 90878 78414.

2. Information we collect

When you submit an enquiry through our website we collect the following personal data:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Your stated interest (apartment type or general enquiry)
  • Any message content you choose to share

We do not collect payment information, government ID, or sensitive personal data through this website.

3. How we use your information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiry about our residential projects
  • Share relevant project updates, pricing, or availability information you have asked for
  • Contact you via phone, email, SMS, or WhatsApp as authorised by your consent at submission

We do not use your information for automated profiling, targeted advertising, or any purpose unrelated to your enquiry.

4. How we store and protect your data

When you submit our enquiry form, the details you enter are used to compose a pre-filled WhatsApp message that opens on your device. No form data is transmitted to our servers, emailed automatically, or sent to any third-party service. The message is only delivered if you choose to press "Send" inside WhatsApp.

Any contact details you subsequently share with us via WhatsApp or phone are stored in our internal records only for as long as necessary to manage your enquiry and any resulting business relationship. We apply reasonable organisational safeguards to prevent unauthorised access.

5. Who we share it with

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party. Your data may be shared only with:

  • Our internal team members handling your enquiry
  • Authorities if required by law

6. DND / NDNC override

By submitting our enquiry form and checking the consent box, you explicitly authorise Rich Land Builders and its representatives to contact you via Email, SMS, WhatsApp, and phone calls. This authorisation may override your DND (Do Not Disturb) or NDNC (National Do Not Call) registration for communications related to your enquiry.

7. Your rights

You may at any time:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Ask us to correct inaccurate information
  • Ask us to delete your details from our records
  • Withdraw consent for future communications

To exercise any of these rights, email us at info@richlandbuilders.com. We will respond within 7 business days.

8. Cookies

This website does not use tracking cookies, analytics scripts, or advertising pixels. No cookie consent banner is required. Google Fonts is loaded via an external CDN request; no personally identifiable data is sent to Google beyond standard IP-level information inherent in any web request.

9. Third-party links

Our website links to Google Maps for location information and to social media profiles. Visiting those links is governed by the respective platform's own privacy policy, not ours.

10. Children's privacy

Our services are directed at adults making residential property enquiries. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under the age of 18.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will reflect any changes. Continued use of the website after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

12. Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact us at info@richlandbuilders.com or write to us at our Coimbatore office address listed above.
