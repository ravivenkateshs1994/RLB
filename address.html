Prime Location & Urban Access

Well‑connected yet tucked away, The Address keeps you close to everyday essentials in Coimbatore.

  • ✈️ Airport Access: Reach Coimbatore International Airport in just 15 minutes by car for effortless travel connections.
  • 🏥 Healthcare Hub: A 10-minute drive brings you to a cluster of clinics and specialty hospitals for peace of mind.
  • 🎓 Education Zone: Top schools and colleges are only 13 minutes away, making daily commutes easy for students.
  • 🛒 Everyday Essentials: Supermarkets, pharmacies, and popular restaurants are within a quick 6-minute drive or auto ride.
  • 🚗 Citywide Connectivity: Central Coimbatore is just 25 minutes by car, and the main railway station is accessible in about 30 minutes, traffic permitting.
