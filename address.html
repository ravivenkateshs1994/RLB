What makes this a True 3 BHK?

In India, many projects are marketed as “3 BHK” but deliver shrunken bedrooms, borrowed light, or poor cross-ventilation — effectively a 2 BHK with an extra room. At The Address, every unit meets a stricter standard:

  • All three bedrooms are full-sized with dedicated natural light and a direct ventilation path
  • No borrowed rooms — every bedroom has its own external window
  • A genuine living area that isn’t a repurposed corridor or passage

What sets this villa apart from a typical 4 BHK home?

Many projects label a stacked apartment floor as a "villa." At The Address, each home is a true independent villa — ground floor through first floor to a private terrace — with Technical Specifications rarely seen at this price point:

  • A dedicated Home Theatre with Dolby Atmos 7.1.2 surround sound, BenQ 4K projector, motorised recliners, and acoustically treated walls — fully installed, not just a room provision
  • Mitsubishi VRF air conditioning across every room including the theatre, with a single common outdoor unit on the terrace — no wall-mounted boxes on facades
  • A private terrace accessible only to your unit, not a shared rooftop
  • 5 kVA solar-hybrid power backup covering lighting and fan circuits throughout the home

  • 🛒 Everyday Essentials: Supermarkets, pharmacies, and popular restaurants are within a quick 6-minute drive or auto ride.
  • 🎓 Education Zone: Top schools and colleges are only 13 minutes away, making daily commutes easy for students.
  • 🏢 Business & IT Parks: TIDEL Park and KGISL Tech Park are less than 15 minutes away, making daily commutes for professionals quick and convenient.
  • 🏥 Healthcare Hub: A 10-minute drive brings you to a cluster of clinics and specialty hospitals for peace of mind.
  • 🏬 Shopping & Leisure: Fun Republic Mall and Prozone Mall are both within a 20-minute drive for shopping, dining, and entertainment.
  • 🌳 Green Spaces: Enjoy easy access to Codissia Trade Fair Complex and the city’s parks for recreation and events.
  • ✈️ Airport Access: Reach Coimbatore International Airport in just 15 minutes by car for effortless travel connections.
  • 🚗 Citywide Connectivity: Central Coimbatore is just 25 minutes by car, and the main railway station is accessible in about 30 minutes, traffic permitting.
