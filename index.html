About Rich Land Builders

Thoughtful homes, built with care and clarity.

Rich Land Builders is a residential-focused team in Coimbatore, bringing empathy, precision, and accountability to every stage of construction.

Our flagship development, The Address, is where this philosophy comes to life: deeper involvement, and complete transparency for every family that chooses us.

Specialist‑led planning & execution Transparent, no‑surprise communication Respect for timelines and commitments
